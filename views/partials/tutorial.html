Warning: If you refresh the page, all entered text will be deleted.

  • To get started, add the prompt for the document.
  • Click on the "Number of Fields" drop down box. The fields will represent the different areas where the essays are graded. Then choose how many fields that were graded and input them in the text box(es). Enter the prompt for your CSV. Hit "Save."
  • After inputing the field names, input the scores for each field. Type the title of the CSV document that you wish. Then copy and paste the text of the document into the text box below.
  • Once you click the "Add" button, you will be able to add another document to add to your CSV document. Once you add a document, a box with the document number will appear.
  • Upon clicking that the document box, you will be able to edit your previous document. You can change the scores and the document in the text box. Once you are done, you can hit the "Replace" button that will appear upon editing to change what you have edited and save it. If you choose not to change the document upon editing, you can click "Cancel" to cancel the editing.
  • Once you are done adding all of your documents to your CSV document, then you can click the "Download CSV" button to download your CSV document.
  • If you wish to start over, you can click the "Reset" button to erase everything.
