To get started, complete basic info about your essay.

Choose an upload format based on the content.

Select the amount of grading criteria you grading on and input the scores for each field (Ex: A-F).

Enter the scores for the grading criteria.

Enter or paste the essay into the text box.

You can add essays into the CSV file by clicking the "Add" button.

You can click any of the numbered boxes under the Text box to edit the text or grade of the essay you select.

When you are completely done with add essays, you can download your CSV file by selecting "Download CSV."

If you wish to delete one of your essays, select the document number box and click the "Delete" button.

You can change the scores and the document in the text box.

Once you are done, select the "Replace" button to save the changes to your grades and text.

If you choose not to change the document upon editing, you can click "Cancel" to cancel the editing.

What is a model? A model is a type of rubric made from LightSide technology to evaluate an essay responding to a specific prompt.
